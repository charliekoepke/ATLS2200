About Me

Hi, my name is Charlie Koepke. I'm a current sophomore in the CTD program at CU Boulder, and I am also minoring in Computer Science. I am originally from Maryland but my family moved to Colorado in 2019. I am a proud adopted Asian (from Korea), and a transgender man.

I'm very interested in graphic design and UI/UX design as well. I also have a passion for music, which is something I've had since I was young. Over the years, I have learned how to play the guitar, piano, violin, and ukulele, and I am always open to learning more instruments. I have also had experience in music production. I took a course in my freshman year of high school, and it is something I enjoy to do as a hobby.
