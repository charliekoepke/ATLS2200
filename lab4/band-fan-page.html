in love with a ghost

about

In Love With a Ghost is a French musical artist that became popular on Soundcloud with their song 'flowers feat. nori'. In Love With a Ghost is active on Twitter, and posts their music to YouTube, Soundcloud, and Bandcamp. In Love With a Ghost goes by they/them pronouns, and even has a song describing how they feel neither like a boy or a girl, titled 'am i a boy? am i a girl? do i really care? i'm hungry anyway'. They have collaborated with fellow Soundcloud artist Snail's House on a couple songs. One is titled 'Interdimensional Portal Leading to a Cute Place', and the other is titled 'Journey', which is available on Snail's House's Soundcloud.
